Green Alien Soup

Description

Sweet and delicious green alien soup to whip up when you want something warm to eat.

Ingredients

  • Green alien
  • Cinnamon
  • Eraser bits

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add in seasonings (cinnamon, eraser bits)
  3. Stir
  4. Add green alien
  5. Boil alien until melted, then serve
